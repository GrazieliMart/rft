Medium Trailer

Medium Refrigerated Trailer

The refrigerated trailer offers a larger storage capacity, making it an efficient solution for preserving chilled and frozen goods.

Ideal for catering services, large events, and businesses that require reliable temporary cold storage, this trailer ensures optimal temperature control and secure transportation of perishable items.

Internal Dimensions: 3m (L) x 1.7m (W)
Door Aperture: 80cm (W)
Max Load Weight: 2000 kg


Door Options

The trailer offers two door aperture options to suit different access needs. The single door measures 800mm wide by 1900mm high, providing a standard entry. The double door option, measuring 1600mm wide by 1820mm high, allows for a wider opening, making it easier to load and unload larger items. These variations ensure practicality and flexibility for different storage and transportation requirements.

Trailers Info

The trailers require a power supply from either a 13A square-pin socket, a 16A round-pin socket, or a generator with a minimum capacity of 10 kW. We provide the necessary cables to connect to any of these power sources.

10kw generator
