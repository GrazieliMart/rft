Rentafridgetrailer was established in 2018 as there was a shortage of affordable refrigerated/freezer trailers for hire.



About Rent a Fridge Trailer

Rentafridgetrailer was founded in 2018 to address the growing demand for affordable refrigerated and freezer trailers for hire in Ireland.

Since then, we have proudly expanded our services to cover most regions across the country. Located in North County Wicklow, our dedicated and friendly team is committed to delivering exceptional service.
We are available to deliver trailers to your location at any time, day or night, ensuring flexibility and convenience for our clients. We specialize in professional and reliable solutions for refrigeration refits, temperature-controlled storage, and on-site backup services, providing critical support when you need it the most.


Our Services and Trailer Options

When you need additional cold storage or freezer space, Rentafridgetrailer offers the perfect solution with our multi-temperature trailers. Whether it’s for a planned event, an unexpected need, or long-term storage, our trailers are designed to cater to private, corporate, and public sector requirements.


Trailer Sizes

We offer three trailer sizes, each equipped with versatile features to meet your refrigeration and freezing needs: Small Trailer, Medium Trailer and Large Trailer.


Additional Information

Our trailers are ideal for both short-term and long-term hire, with removable shelving to suit your specific storage needs.
Whether you’re hosting an event or require backup storage during a refrigeration refit, our trailers provide flexible and reliable cold storage solutions.
Please note: It is the hirer’s responsibility to arrange full comprehensive insurance cover for the equipment throughout the hire period.
