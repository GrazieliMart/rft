Rent a Fridge Trailer Website & Personal Privacy Statement

Updated: January 2025

1. Acknowledgement and acceptance of terms

At Rent a Fridge Trailer, transparency and the security of your data are our priorities. This Privacy Statement outlines our practices regarding the collection and use of information when you interact with our website or our customer service. By accessing rentafridgetrailer.com or contacting Rent a Fridge Trailer, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to the practices described in this Privacy Statement, consenting to the collection and use of information as outlined here.



2. Information We Collect and How We Use It



Internet Information

We collect basic information related to your use of our site in order to improve your experience and provide personalized service. Examples of information we may collect include:

  • Your Internet Protocol (IP) address.
  • The type of browser or device you are using.
  • The number of links you click on our site.
  • The geographic location from which you accessed the site.
  • The date and time of your visit.
  • Your Internet Service Provider's name.
  • The webpage you linked from to our site.
  • Pages you viewed on the site.

This information is used solely for the purpose of improving our website’s performance and enhancing the services we offer. Rent a Fridge Trailer does not share, sell, rent, or lend your personal information to third parties. We use your data exclusively for contact purposes, sending quotes, service-related offers, and communication regarding your request.



Information Collected via Phone and In-Person Meetings

When you contact us via phone or through our sales or customer service teams, we collect necessary information to fulfill your request, such as:

  • Company name.
  • Your name.
  • Billing and shipping address.
  • Credit card information.
  • Phone number.
  • Email address.
  • Bank and reference information.

This information is used solely for the requested transaction and to ensure that your experience with us is efficient and transparent. Rent a Fridge Trailer does not store credit card information for future billing; the information is collected only during the transaction and used exclusively for the request at hand.

Your continued use of our services constitutes your agreement to this Privacy Statement and any updates.
