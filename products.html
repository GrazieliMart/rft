Sizes Avaiable

Rent a Fridge Trailer offers three sizes available: 2m, 3m (double or single doors) and 4m unit.

2m Trailer

  • Door aperture: 750mm wide x 1.74m high
  • Internal dimension: 2.5m x 1.5m x 2m
  • Cubic capacity: 7.25 m³
  • Gross weight: 2000kg
  • Payload: 1200kg

3m Trailer

  • Door aperture: Single 800mm wide x 1900mm high or Double 1600mm wide x 1820mm high
  • Internal dimension: 3m x 1.8m x 2m
  • Cubic capacity: 10.8 m³
  • Gross weight: 3500kg
  • Payload: 2400kg

4m Trailer

  • Door aperture: 800mm wide x 1900mm high
  • Internal dimension: 3.7m x 1.6m x 2m
  • Cubic capacity: 11.6 m³
  • Gross weight: 3500kg
  • Payload: 2500kg

About the trailers

Our trailers are simple to operate and meet all current food and hygiene regulations. All of our units are racked with removable shelving, are secured with a hitch lock and a wheel clamp. They require power from either a domestic 13amp socket or a 4kva generator with all the cables provided by us.

Lockable rear door

Keys
