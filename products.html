Sizes Avaiable

Rent a Fridge Trailer offers three sizes available: 2m, 3m (double or single doors) and 4m unit.

Small Trailer

  • Door aperture: 750mm wide x 1.74m high
  • Internal dimensions: 2m x 1.2m x 1.5m
  • Cubic capacity: 3.6 m³
  • Gross weight: 1500kg
  • Payload: 1000kg

Medium Trailer

  • Door aperture: Single 800mm wide x 1.9m high or Double 1600mm wide x 1.82m high
  • Internal dimensions: 3m x 1.8m x 2m
  • Cubic capacity: 10.8 m³
  • Gross weight: 2500kg
  • Payload: 1800kg

Large Trailer

  • Door aperture: 800mm wide x 1.9m high
  • Internal dimensions: 4m x 2m x 2.2m
  • Cubic capacity: 17.6 m³
  • Gross weight: 3500kg
  • Payload: 2500kg

About the trailers

Our trailers are simple to operate and meet all current food and hygiene regulations. All of our units are racked with removable shelving, are secured with a hitch lock and a wheel clamp. They require power from either a domestic 13amp socket or a 4kva generator with all the cables provided by us.
