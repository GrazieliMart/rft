4m Trailer

4m Refrigerated Trailer

The 4-meter refrigerated trailer offers an even larger storage capacity, making it an ideal choice for businesses and events that require substantial temporary cold storage. With an efficient design, this trailer ensures optimal temperature control for both chilled and frozen goods, providing reliable and secure transportation for perishable items.

Internal Dimensions: 3.7m (L) x 1.6m (W) x 2m (H)
Cubic Capacity: 11.6 m³
Door Aperture: 800mm (W) x 1900mm (H)
Gross Weight: 3500 kg
Payload Capacity: 2500 kg


4m Trailer and Door Options

The 4-meter refrigerated trailer is designed to provide a larger storage capacity, offering 11.6 m³ of space for both chilled and frozen goods. Ideal for large events, catering services, and businesses that require significant temporary cold storage, this trailer ensures optimal temperature control and reliable transportation for perishable items.

10kw generator

The trailers require power from either a 13 amp square pin or 16amp round pin socket or a minimum 10 kw generator - We supply cables to connect to either supply.
